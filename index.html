United States Citizenship Practice Test

This is a practice test for the Naturalization process in which a Resident Alien becomes a United States Citizen.

You will have to enter answers into all fields provided by the test and can only move forward after answering the question whether its correct or incorrect

You will be required to type the answers into the fields. Spelling is important! Do not include extra punctuation the following characters arent necessary "",./? Capitalization will not count against you in this test, although it is best practice to to ensure your answers are capitalized if they need to be.

This test is meant to be used as practice, all questions and answers are provided by USCIS.GOV
